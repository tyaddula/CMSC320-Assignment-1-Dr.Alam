Q3: What is the difference between merging and rebasing?

Answer: Merging combines changes from one branch into another creating a new commit called 
merge commit. It has two parent commits where one is from the current branch and the other 
is from the branch being merged. Rebase moves a sequence of commits to a new base commit. It 
applies commits from one branch to the top of another branch.
